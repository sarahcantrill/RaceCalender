About Us

Pro Racing was founded by former endurance racer James Calloway in 2010 with a vision to dominate the endurance racing scene. Over the years, we have built a reputation as a force to be reckoned with, securing numerous wins and podium finishes in the most prestigious endurance races across the globe.

Our headquarters in Silverstone, UK, houses a state-of-the-art facility where engineering meets passion, and every component of our race cars is fine-tuned to perfection. With a team of world-class engineers, strategists, and mechanics, we leave nothing to chance.

We race with heart, discipline, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. Every season, we aim to push the limits of performance, ensuring that we stay ahead of the competition and continue to set new benchmarks in endurance racing.

Events

Pro Racing competes in some of the most demanding endurance racing events worldwide. Our calendar is packed with high-stakes races where every lap counts and every pit stop is crucial.

We regularly compete in the FIA World Endurance Championship, the IMSA WeatherTech SportsCar Championship, and endurance classics such as the 24 Hours of Daytona and the Spa 24 Hours.

Our crowning achievement is our success at the 24 Hours of Le Mans, where we have secured multiple podium finishes, proving our dominance in the endurance racing arena. Each event presents a new challenge, testing the limits of our cars and drivers while showcasing the relentless spirit of Pro Racing.

Stay updated with our racing calendar and witness the high-speed action as we take on the world's best endurance teams.

Drivers

Pro Racing is home to some of the most talented and experienced drivers in endurance racing. Each member of our lineup brings a unique skill set, ensuring we have the perfect balance of speed, strategy, and endurance.

Leading the charge is veteran endurance racer Alex Hartmann, a former Le Mans champion known for his razor-sharp precision and race-winning instincts.

Alongside him is rising star Daniel Riva, whose raw talent and fearless overtakes make him a formidable competitor.

Completing our lineup is endurance specialist Sophia Langley, a master of consistency and tire management, whose experience in 12-hour and 24-hour races makes her an invaluable asset to the team.

Together, our drivers form an unstoppable force, pushing each other to achieve greatness and bring home victory for Pro Racing.

Our Cars

Pro Racing’s success is built on a foundation of cutting-edge racing machines designed for endurance, speed, and reliability. Our fleet consists of meticulously engineered prototypes and GT cars, each optimized for the challenges of endurance racing.

Our flagship prototype, the PR-01 Hypercar, boasts a hybrid powertrain delivering over 800 horsepower, advanced aerodynamics, and a lightweight carbon fiber chassis. It has dominated the endurance racing scene, setting lap records and proving its superiority in the toughest conditions.

In the GT class, our PR-GT3 model, based on the legendary European supercar platform, features a naturally aspirated V10 engine, race-tuned suspension, and a flawless balance of speed and durability. It has earned multiple class wins and remains a fan favorite on the endurance racing circuit.

At Pro Racing, we push the boundaries of automotive technology, ensuring that our cars remain at the forefront of endurance racing performance. Join us as we continue to redefine speed and endurance on the world’s most demanding tracks.
