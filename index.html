About Us

Pro Racing was founded by former endurance racer James Calloway in 2010 with a vision to dominate the endurance racing scene. Over the years, we have built a reputation as a force to be reckoned with, securing numerous wins and podium finishes in the most prestigious endurance races across the globe.

Our headquarters in Silverstone, UK, houses a state-of-the-art facility where engineering meets passion, and every component of our race cars is fine-tuned to perfection. With a team of world-class engineers, strategists, and mechanics, we leave nothing to chance.

We race with heart, discipline, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. Every season, we aim to push the limits of performance, ensuring that we stay ahead of the competition and continue to set new benchmarks in endurance racing.

Events

Pro Racing competes in some of the most demanding endurance racing events worldwide. Our calendar is packed with high-stakes races where every lap counts and every pit stop is crucial.

We regularly compete in the FIA World Endurance Championship, the IMSA WeatherTech SportsCar Championship, and endurance classics such as the 24 Hours of Daytona and the Spa 24 Hours.

Our crowning achievement is our success at the 24 Hours of Le Mans, where we have secured multiple podium finishes, proving our dominance in the endurance racing arena. Each event presents a new challenge, testing the limits of our cars and drivers while showcasing the relentless spirit of Pro Racing.

Stay updated with our racing calendar and witness the high-speed action as we take on the world's best endurance teams.
